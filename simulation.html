How to Demonstrate Overfitting:

  1. Increase the Model Complexity to get the Training Error as low as possible.
  2. You might think you have a perfect model, but you are only looking at the training data.
  3. Click 'Reveal Test Set'. Now you see the model's true performance on unseen data. Notice how a low training error can lead to a very high Test Error. This is overfitting!
  4. Use 'New Data' to see if your model generalizes well.
